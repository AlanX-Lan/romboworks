Dyna.AI Unveils Agent Studio: A Powerful AI Agent Builder Platform at Singapore Fintech Festival

Dyna.AI Agent Studio, ModelGPT, AgentGPT, and FS (Financial Services Industry Suite) were products exhibited at the booth.

Singapore, 27 November, 2024

Agent Studio is a groundbreaking AI agent builder platform built upon Dyna.AI’s proprietary large language model (LLM). The platform enables organizations to quickly and efficiently create policy-driven, AI-powered conversational agents capable of handling various complex tasks. It offers solutions for customer support, financial services, and more, transforming the way businesses operate while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Agent Studio addresses specific pain points for industry leaders by offering a flexible solution that includes APIs, a no-code platform, and an intuitive UI for deployment. With the Financial Services Industry Suite (FS), it adds layers of security and compliance, ensuring seamless integration with existing business workflows.

Cynthia Sari, the Head of Business Development at Dyna.AI, commented, “While there is certainly a lot of interest in AI adoption within Singapore’s financial sector, many organizations still face challenges in creating AI-driven solutions that are both scalable and compliant.”

Cynthia Sari, Head of Business Development, Dyna.AI
